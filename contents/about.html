About Me

Welcome to my world of creativity and exploration!

I'm Payel Das, a passionate Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student at Chaudhary Charan Singh University (CCSU), on a mission to merge technology and creativity. As a web and front-end developer, I specialize in crafting visually appealing and user-friendly websites that not only look great but work seamlessly.

Each project I undertake is a blend of my passion for front-end development and design. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the latest frameworks to bring fresh ideas to life, always with a focus on detail, usability, and innovation.

When I’m not building the web, I’m exploring the latest design trends, playing with new tech tools, or seeking inspiration in art, culture, and nature. I’m a firm believer in continuous learning and love connecting with fellow tech enthusiasts to share knowledge and ideas.
